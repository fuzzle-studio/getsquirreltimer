Privacy Policy for Squirrel Timer

Last Updated: 2026

At Squirrel Timer, we take the privacy of our users, especially children, very seriously.

  • Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • Third Parties: We do not share data with third-party services.
  • Children's Privacy: Our app is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and does not track users.
  • Permissions: The app only requests necessary local device permissions required for timer functionality and does not access personal files or contacts.
