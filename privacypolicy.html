Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

At Squirrel Timer, we take the privacy of our users, especially children, very seriously. Designed for calm transitions, our app ensures a safe digital environment.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

2. Third Parties

We do not share data with any third-party analytics or advertising services.

3. Children's Privacy

Our application is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and does not track users or request unnecessary permissions.

4. App Permissions

The app only requests the minimum device functions necessary to operate the timer and sound features. It does not access your personal data, contacts, or location.
